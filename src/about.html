About SLBT Safe Fleet

At SLBT Safe Fleet, we are committed to ensuring your fleet remains on the road, fulfilling its purpose with minimal downtime. Originating as a new division of the reputable St. Lucie Battery & Tire (SLBT), we bring a legacy of excellence and a promise of quality service tailored to meet the distinct needs of trucking and transportation companies.

Our Journey

Recognizing the unique challenges faced by the fleet industry, SLBT envisioned a dedicated division to provide specialized services. Thus, SLBT Safe Fleet was born, with a singular focus on delivering reliable, comprehensive fleet services. Our journey is driven by a passion for excellence and a relentless pursuit to provide value and satisfaction to our clients.
